START of the ATLANTIS PROJECT

I was born in Poland in the last century. Inventiveness is my life passion. I’m lucky that I don’t have to stick to just one scientific discipline. When an idea occurs to me, I don’t think whether I have the sufficient knowledge on the subject. I investigate, learn, and discover the problem by myself, and then I refer to the literature. What does it give me? Only that I won’t learn “beforehand” that it is impossible, and that I sometimes manage to find a new solution.

Nature bestowed me with a gift
of seeing the world differently, for
which I’m very grateful to it.
